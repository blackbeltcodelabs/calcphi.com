Frequently Asked Questions

What is the current KVP interest rate?+
The current KVP interest rate is 7.5% per annum for Q1 FY 2026-27, compounded annually. At 7.5%, money doubles in approximately 115 months. The rate is revised quarterly by the government.
Is KVP a good investment?+
KVP is safe (sovereign guarantee) but tax-inefficient — interest is fully taxable at your slab rate. PPF at 7.1% is better for tax-efficient investors since PPF interest is tax-free. KVP suits investors who need a simple doubling scheme without TDS deduction.
