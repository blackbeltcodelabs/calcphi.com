Frequently Asked Questions

What charges apply on stock trading in India?+
Delivery trades: STT 0.1% buy+sell, brokerage 0-0.5%, GST 18% on brokerage, SEBI 0.0001%, stamp duty 0.015% on buy. Intraday: STT 0.025% on sell only. These charges matter most for short-term traders where margins are thin.
What is STCG and LTCG on stocks India?+
STCG (under 12 months): 20% flat. LTCG (over 12 months): 12.5% on gains above ₹1.25L exemption/year. F&O gains: taxed as business income at slab rate.
