Plan Your Repayment Before You Borrow

Education loans have a moratorium period (course + 6-12 months) during which no EMI is paid. Interest still accrues. Know your true burden before you borrow ₹20-50 lakhs.

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N-1]. Moratorium: typically course duration + 6 months. Interest accrues during moratorium and is capitalised — increasing your effective loan amount.
