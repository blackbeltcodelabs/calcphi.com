₹50,000 SIP — Wealth Building at Scale

How much will ₹50,000 SIP give in 20 years?+
At 12% annual return, ₹50,000/month SIP for 20 years gives approximately ₹4.99 Crore. You invest ₹1.2 Crore (₹50,000 × 240 months) and earn ₹3.79 Crore in returns — a 316% absolute return. This is true wealth creation through disciplined long-term SIP.
What corpus does ₹50,000 SIP build in 10 years?+
At 12% annual return, ₹50,000/month SIP for 10 years gives approximately ₹1.15 Crore. You invest ₹60 lakhs and earn ₹55 lakhs in returns. At 15%, the corpus grows to ₹1.37 Crore. The 10-year SIP still shows significant compounding despite the shorter horizon.
How should I split ₹50,000 SIP across funds?+
For ₹50,000 monthly SIP, a diversified allocation: ₹20,000 in Nifty 50 index fund, ₹10,000 in Nifty Next 50 index fund, ₹10,000 in flexi-cap active fund, ₹7,500 in international/US index fund, ₹2,500 in gold fund (as a hedge). This gives domestic + international diversification with low overall expense ratio.
Can ₹50,000 SIP help me retire early?+
Yes. ₹50,000/month SIP at 12% for 15 years builds approximately ₹2.5 Crore. Using the 4% safe withdrawal rate, this generates ₹1 lakh/month in retirement income. For most Indians under 45 starting this SIP, FIRE (Financial Independence Retire Early) is achievable by 55–60 with this level of commitment and discipline.
What is the LTCG tax impact on ₹50,000 SIP?+
For equity mutual funds held over 1 year, LTCG tax is 10% on gains above ₹1 lakh per financial year. At ₹50,000/month SIP with 12% returns, annual gains after year 5 can be ₹8–15 lakhs. Strategic SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan) can manage LTCG liability — withdraw up to ₹1 lakh in gains per year tax-free. Consult a tax advisor for optimal withdrawal planning.
