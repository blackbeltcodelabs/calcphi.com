Frequently Asked Questions

How are ESOPs taxed in India?+
Stage 1 Exercise: (FMV - Exercise price) × shares = perquisite income, taxed at slab up to 31.2%. TDS by employer. Stage 2 Sale: LTCG if held 24+ months from exercise at 12.5% above ₹1.25L. STCG at 20% if shorter.
When should I exercise my ESOPs?+
Exercise when you have deductions to offset perquisite tax, when appreciation ahead is significant, or before company IPO/acquisition. Spread across years to use multiple ₹1.25L LTCG exemptions. Always consult a CA for large ESOP grants.
