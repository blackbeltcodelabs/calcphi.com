Frequently Asked Questions

How is bonus taxed in India?+
Bonus is added to total annual taxable income and taxed at marginal slab rate. In 30% slab: 30% + 4% cess = 31.2% tax. Employers deduct TDS in the month bonus is paid, often at 30.9% regardless of your actual slab.
Can I reduce tax on my bonus?+
Yes — maximise 80C, NPS 80CCD(1B), HRA, and other deductions in the year you receive the bonus. If your salary already uses most deductions, consider timing bonus receipt toward year-end when remaining deduction space is clear.
