₹15,000 SIP — Key Questions

How much does ₹15,000/month SIP give in 20 years?+
At 12% annual returns, ₹15,000/month SIP for 20 years builds a corpus of approximately ₹1.74Cr over 20 years. The power of compounding means that your returns in the last 5 years often exceed your entire first 15 years of accumulated wealth.
Which fund is best for ₹15,000 monthly SIP?+
For ₹15,000/month, a Nifty 50 index fund is the most sensible core holding — low expense ratio (0.1-0.2%), automatic diversification, and historically reliable returns. Once corpus crosses ₹5 lakhs, consider adding a Nifty Next 50 or flexi-cap fund as a second holding.
Should I increase my ₹15,000 SIP over time?+
Yes — a 10% annual step-up on ₹15,000/month dramatically increases your final corpus without requiring a lump sum. Link SIP increases to your annual salary hike. Even increasing from ₹15,000 to ₹16,500 in year 2 can add significantly to your 20-year corpus.
