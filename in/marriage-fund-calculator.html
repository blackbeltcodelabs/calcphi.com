Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an Indian wedding cost in 2026?+
Tier 2 city modest: ₹5-15L. Tier 1 city mid-size: ₹20-50L. Large wedding: ₹50L-2Cr. With 7-8% annual inflation in wedding costs, plan at least 3-5 years ahead. A wedding loan at 14% adds ₹7L interest to a ₹15L wedding.
Should I take a personal loan for a wedding?+
Avoid it. A ₹15L wedding loan at 14% for 3 years costs ₹22L total. Better: SIP ₹15,000/month for 4 years at 12% = ₹9.3L. Supplement with family contributions. Start early and avoid starting married life in debt.
