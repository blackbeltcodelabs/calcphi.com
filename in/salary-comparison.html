Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compare two salary offers in India?+
Compare: fixed monthly take-home, employer PF contribution, variable pay certainty, ESOP value, health insurance, and tax efficiency. A ₹12L well-structured CTC can give higher in-hand than a ₹14L poorly structured one.
What is the difference between CTC and gross salary?+
CTC includes everything the employer spends including employer PF and gratuity provision. Gross salary = CTC minus employer PF and gratuity. Net in-hand = Gross minus employee PF, TDS, and professional tax. Typically 65-75% of CTC.
