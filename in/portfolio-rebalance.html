Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I rebalance my portfolio?+
Annually or when any asset class drifts more than 5% from your target allocation. Annual rebalancing has historically added 0.3-0.5% extra return vs no rebalancing, simply through systematic buy-low-sell-high discipline.
What is the tax implication of rebalancing?+
Each rebalancing sale triggers capital gains tax. Use new contributions to rebalance where possible (buy the underperforming asset with fresh money) to avoid selling and triggering tax. If you must sell, preferentially sell assets held 12+ months to access LTCG rates.
