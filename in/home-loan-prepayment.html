Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to prepay a home loan?+
The first 5 years are most impactful — when most of your EMI goes to interest. Prepaying in years 1-7 saves significantly more than prepaying in year 15-18. Floating rate home loans have zero prepayment penalty by RBI regulation.
Does prepayment affect Section 24 tax deduction?+
Yes — less loan outstanding means less interest paid, so your Section 24 deduction (up to ₹2L for self-occupied) reduces. At 30% slab, factor this in when calculating net savings from prepayment.
