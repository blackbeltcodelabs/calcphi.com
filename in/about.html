About CalcPhi

φ
Phi (φ = 1.618...) — the golden ratio. The mathematical principle behind perfect proportions, found in nature, art, and compounding wealth.

CalcPhi is a free financial calculator platform built by Black Belt Code Labs. Our mission is simple: give every Indian investor access to clear, accurate, and beautifully designed financial tools — completely free, no sign-up required.

Why We Built CalcPhi

Most financial calculators online are either ugly, inaccurate, cluttered with ads, or buried inside apps that require registration. We wanted to build something different — fast, honest tools that show you real numbers and help you make better financial decisions.

The name CalcPhi comes from the golden ratio (φ = 1.618...), which represents the kind of compounding growth that smart, patient investing creates. Just as phi appears naturally in growth patterns across nature, compound interest creates naturally accelerating wealth over time.

Our Principles

  • Free forever. Every calculator on CalcPhi is and will remain completely free.
  • No registration. We never ask for your email or personal details.
  • Your data stays with you. All calculations happen in your browser. Nothing you type is sent to our servers.
  • Accurate math. We use standard financial formulas — the same ones used by SEBI-registered advisors and financial textbooks.
  • No guarantees. Our tools provide estimates for planning purposes. Always consult a SEBI-registered financial advisor before making investment decisions.
